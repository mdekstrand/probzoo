## Parameters

Location
:   $\mu \in \Reals$, the mean

Scale
:   $\sigma > 0$, the standard deviation

This yields the PDF:

$$
p(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma}}
$$

And distribution function:

$$
\P[X \le x] = F(x) = \frac{1}{2}\large[1 + erf(\frac{x - \mu}{\sigma \sqrt{2}}\large]
$$
